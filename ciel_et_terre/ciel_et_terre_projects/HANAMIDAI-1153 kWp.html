This floating solar plant of 1153,11 kWp is installed on a rain water retention pond, located near Higashimatsuyama city, Saitama prefecture, Kanto, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4522 panels (255Wp KYOCERA modules), and covers about 85,71 % of the water surface (1,26 out of 1,47 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 4,9 m and a level variation of 4,9 m.

The construction lasted 13 weeks and the grid connection was effective in August 2015.
