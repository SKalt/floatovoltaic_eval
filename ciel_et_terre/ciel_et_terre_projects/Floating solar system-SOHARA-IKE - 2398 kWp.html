This floating solar plant of 2397,60 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Matsusaka city, Mie prefecture, Chubu, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 8880 panels (270 Wp MITSUBISHI modules), and covers about 68,89 % of the water surface (2,48 out of 3,60 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 1,8 m and a level variation of 1,8 m.

The construction lasted 20 weeks and the grid connection was effective in March 2016.
