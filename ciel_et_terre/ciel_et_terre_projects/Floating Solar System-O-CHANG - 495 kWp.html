This floating solar plant of 494,5 kWp is installed on an irrigation dam, located in Cheongwon district, Chungcheonbuk, South Korea.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 1978 panels (250Wp HANWHA modules), and covers about 1,14 % of the water surface (0,56 out of 49,12 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with deadweight was designed to meet a maximum depth of 93,8 m and a level variation of 8,3 m.

The construction lasted 2 weeks and the grid connection was effective in February 2015.
