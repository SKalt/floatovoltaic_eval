This floating solar plant of 300 kWp is installed on an water storage pond, located in Fukuoka city, Fukuoka prefecture, Kansai, Japan

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 1 200 panels (250 Wp ZNShine Solar modules), and covers about 44 % of the water surface (0,35 out of 0,79 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5,3 m and a level variation of 5,3 m.

The construction lasted 6 weeks and the grid connection was effective in July 2016.
