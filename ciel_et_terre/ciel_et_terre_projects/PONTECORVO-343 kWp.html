This floating solar plant of 343,20 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Sant’Ermete, Province of Savona, Italy.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 1320 panels (260 Wp PEIMAR modules), and covers about 43,18 % of the water surface (0,38 out of 0,88 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5,5 m and a level variation of 5,5 m.

The construction lasted 9 weeks and the grid connection was effective in March 2016.
