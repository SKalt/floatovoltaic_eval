This floating solar plant of 2870,28 kWp is installed on two irrigation ponds, located near Takaoka city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 11256 panels (255Wp KYOCERA modules), and covers about 59,28 % of the water surface (3,13 out of 5,28 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5,7 m and a level variation of 5,7 m.

The construction lasted 15 weeks and the grid connection was effective in March 2015.
