This floating solar plant of 808,50 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Fukuzaki district, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 3234 panels (250 Wp SHARP modules), and covers about 28,13 % of the water surface (0,92 out of 3,27 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 4,4 m and a level variation of 4,4 m.

The construction lasted 11 weeks and the grid connection was effective in February 2016.
