This floating solar plant of 2449 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Susuka city, Mie prefecture, Japan

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 9072 panels (270 Wp KYOCERA modules), and covers about 27 % of the water surface (2.48 out of 9.21 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 7.2 m and a level variation of 7.2 m.

The construction lasted 8 weeks and the grid connection was effective in July 2016.
