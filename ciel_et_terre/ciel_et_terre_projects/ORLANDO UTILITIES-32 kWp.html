This floating solar plant of 32 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Orlando, the U.S.A.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 100 panels (315 Wp RENESOLA modules), and covers about 2.90 % of the water surface (0.44 out of 1.22 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was EPC (Engineering – Procurement and Construction). We were in charge of Hydrelio® supply, solar island and anchoring systems design and installation. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 3.0 m and a level variation of 0.45 m.

The grid connection will be effective in February 2017.
