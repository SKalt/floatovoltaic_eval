This floating solar plant of 1212 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Kobe city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4752 panels (255 Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 15 % of the water surface (1.29 out of 8.44 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5,6 m and a level variation of 3 m.

The construction lasted 8 weeks and the grid connection was effective in May 2016.
