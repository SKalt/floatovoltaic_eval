This floating Solar PV system of 1485 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Himeji city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar PV system supports 5940 panels (250Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 14,49 % of the water surface (1,69 out of 11,66 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 8,3 m and a level variation of 8,3 m.

The construction lasted 6 weeks and the grid connection was effective in September 2015.
