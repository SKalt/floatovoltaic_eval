This floating solar plant of 270 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Malaysia.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 900 panels (300 Wp BLUESUN modules), and covers about 1.5 % of the water surface (0.27 out of 17.94 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio® supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 45.6 m and a level variation of 17.5 m.

The grid connection was effective in November 2016.
