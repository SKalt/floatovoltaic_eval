This floating solar plant of 108 kWp is installed on a water treatment pond, located in Sepang city, Selango, Malaysia.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 432 panels (250 Wp ET SOLAR modules), and covers about 3,57 % of the water surface (0,15 out of 4,20 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply and solar island design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 27 m and a level variation of 20 m.

The construction lasted 1 week and the grid connection was effective in November 2015.
