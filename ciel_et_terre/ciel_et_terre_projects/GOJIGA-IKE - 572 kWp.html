This floating solar plant of 572 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Hyogo prefecture, Japan.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 2200 panels (260 Wp JINKO modules), and covers about 28 % of the water surface (0.64 out of 2.32 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio® supply, and solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 2.4 m and a level variation of 2.4 m.

The grid connection was effective in January 2017.
