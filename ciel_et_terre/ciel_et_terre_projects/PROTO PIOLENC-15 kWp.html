This floating solar plant of 15,04 kWp is installed on a quarry lake, located in Piolenc city, near Orange, in the south of France.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 64 panels (230 & 235Wp GESOLAR modules), and covers about 0,04 % of the water surface (0,016 out of 37,15 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design, supply & installation. The bottom anchoring with deadweight was designed to meet a maximum depth of 11,8 m and a level variation of 5 m.

The construction lasted 1 week and the auto-consumption was effective in 2011.

This project was the first pilot using Hydrelio©.
