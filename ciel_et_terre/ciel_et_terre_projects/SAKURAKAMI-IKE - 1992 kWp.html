This floating solar plant of 1992 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Hyogo prefecture, Japan.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 7810 panels (255 Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 35 % of the water surface (2.49 out of 7.04 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio® supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 8.4 m and a level variation of 8.4 m.

The grid connection was effective in December 2016.
