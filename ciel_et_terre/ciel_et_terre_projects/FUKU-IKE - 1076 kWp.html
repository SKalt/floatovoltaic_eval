This floating solar plant of 1076,4 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Minamiawaji city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4032 panels (260Wp MITSUBISHI modules), and covers about 71,61 % of the water surface (1,11 out of 1,55 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 2 m and a level variation of 2 m.

The construction lasted 10 weeks and the grid connection was effective in June 2015.

This project was constructed on the dried pond.
