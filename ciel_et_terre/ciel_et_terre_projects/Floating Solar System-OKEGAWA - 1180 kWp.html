This floating solar plant of 1179,36 kWp is installed on a rain water retention pond, located in Okegawa city, Saitama prefecture, Kanto, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4536 panels (260Wp JA SOLAR modules), and covers about 37,79 % of the water surface (1,16 out of 3,07 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design & supply. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 6 m and a level variation of 6 m.

The construction lasted 6 weeks and the grid connection was effective in July 2013.

This project was the first floating mega solar plant in Japan.
