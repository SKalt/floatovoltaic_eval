This floating solar plant of 100 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Benacre village,  Suffolk, UK.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 400 panels (250 Wp REC modules), and covers about 1,58 % of the water surface (0,11 out of 6,97 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design, supply & installation. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5,5 m and a level variation of 5,5 m.

The construction lasted 2 weeks and the grid connection was effective in December 2015.
