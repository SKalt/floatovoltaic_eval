This floating solar plant of 1751 kWp is installed on an irrigation reservoir, located in Hyogo prefecture, Japan.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 7005 panels (250 Wp SHARP modules), and covers about 54 % of the water surface (1.92 out of 3.53 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio® supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 7.8 m and a level variation of 5.1 m.

The grid connection was effective in September 2016.
