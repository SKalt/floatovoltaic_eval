This floating solar plant of 1078 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Fukuzaki district, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4312 panels (250 Wp SHARP modules), and covers about 18,95 % of the water surface (1,19 out of 6,28 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 4,8 m and a level variation of 4,8 m.

The construction lasted 15 weeks and the grid connection was effective in March 2016.
