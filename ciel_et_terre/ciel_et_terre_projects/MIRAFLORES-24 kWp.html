This floating solar plant of 24 kWp is installed on a water retention pond, the Panama Canal, in Panama.

The Hydrelio® floating solar system supports 96 panels (250 Wp TRINA modules), and covers about 0.2 % of the water surface (0.03 out of 14.4 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio® supply, and solar island design and anchoring system design.

The grid connection was effective in February 2017.
