This floating solar plant of 1097,6 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Yasugi city, Shimane prefecture, Chugoku, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4480 panels (245Wp KYOCERA modules), and covers about 22,16 % of the water surface (1,25 out of 5,64 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply and solar island design. The bottom anchoring with deadweight was designed to meet a maximum depth of 2,4 m and a level variation of 2,4 m.

The construction lasted 13 weeks and the grid connection was effective in November 2014.
