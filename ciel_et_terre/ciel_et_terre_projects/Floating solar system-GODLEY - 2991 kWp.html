This floating solar plant of 2990,79 kWp is installed on a water treatment pond, located in Godley city, Greater Manchester, England.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 10494 panels (285Wp JA SOLAR modules), and covers about 47,68 % of the water surface (2,78 out of 5,83 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design, supply & installation. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 9,9 m and a level variation of 9,9 m.

The construction lasted 15 weeks and the grid connection was effective in January 2016.
