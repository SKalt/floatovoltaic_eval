This floating solar plant of 1428 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in  Inami town, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 5712 panels (250 Wp SHARP modules), and covers about 23,11 % of the water surface (1,59 out of 6,88 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 6,4 m and a level variation of 6,4 m.

The construction lasted 12 weeks and the grid connection was effective in December 2015.
