This floating solar plant of 5,39 kWp is installed on an aquaculture pond, located in Samut Songkhram province, Thailand.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 22 panels (245Wp KYOCERA modules), and covers about 2,94 % of the water surface (0,01 out of 0,34 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply and solar island design. The shores anchoring was designed to meet a maximum depth of 5 m and a level variation of 5 m.

The construction lasted 3 days and the grid connection was effective in October 2014.
