This floating solar plant of 1008 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located near Ono city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 4032 panels (250Wp TRINA modules), and covers about 72,15 % of the water surface (1,14 out of 1,58 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 4,7 m and a level variation of 4,7 m.

The construction lasted 6 weeks and the grid connection was effective in May 2015.
