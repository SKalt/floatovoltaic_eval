This floating solar plant of 200 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located near Wargrave city, Berkshire, United kingdom.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 800 panels (250Wp TRINA modules), and covers about 14,09 % of the water surface (0,21 out of 1,49 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design & supply. The shores anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 6,1 m and a level variation of 6,1 m.

The construction lasted 6 days and the grid connection was effective in October 2014.

This project was the first floating solar plant in the UK. This system generates renewable energy on-site to power the reservoir’s pump for the farm irrigation, and enables Sheeplands Farm to increase their energy independence without using up valuable land space. The installation also benefits from the government’s Feed-in-Tariff.
