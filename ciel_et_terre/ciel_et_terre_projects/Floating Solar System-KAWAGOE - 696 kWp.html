Floating Solar System – KAWAGOE – 696,15 kWp – a rain water retention pond in Kawagoe city, Saitama prefecture, Kanto, Japan – Hydrelio© – Ciel & Terre

This floating solar plant of 696,15 kWp is installed on a rain water retention pond, located in Kawagoe city, Saitama prefecture, Kanto, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 2730 panels (255Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 37,74 % of the water surface (0,8 out of 2,12 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 3 m and a level variation of 3 m.

The construction lasted 5 weeks and the grid connection was effective in June 2014.
