This floating solar plant of 848 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond for rice agriculture, located in Ono city, Hyogo prefecture, Kansai, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 3392 panels (250Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 40,33 % of the water surface (0,98 out of 2,43 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 2 m and a level variation of 2 m.

The construction lasted 10 weeks and the grid connection was effective in September 2014.
