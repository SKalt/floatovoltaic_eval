This floating solar plant of 471,24 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located in Gringley-on-the-hill village, South Yorkshire, UK.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 1848 panels (255 Wp REC modules), and covers about 10,57 % of the water surface (0,5 out of 4,73 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 3,9 m and a level variation of 3,9 m.

The construction lasted 4 weeks and the grid connection was effective in December 2015.
