This floating solar plant of 7750,4 kWp is installed on an irrigation pond, located near Higashimatsuyama city, Saitama prefecture, Kanto, Japan.

The Hydrelio© floating solar system supports 27456 panels (275Wp YINGLI modules), and covers about 57,46 % of the water surface (7,43 out of 12,93 ha).

For this project, Ciel & Terre was in charge of Hydrelio© supply, solar island design and anchoring system design. The bottom anchoring with percussive driven earth anchors was designed to meet a maximum depth of 6,9 m and a level variation of 6,9 m.

The construction lasted 22 weeks and the grid connection was effective in October 2015.
